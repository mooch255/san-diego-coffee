The passion and obsession of the r/SanDiegoCoffeeBeans community is the reason this exists. What started as conversation became an interactive map.

Every Hidden Gem, Mapped

Roasters and specialty cafés mapped across San Diego neighborhoods. Filter, browse, and find your next go-to spot.

Community Input & Expertise

We rely on the knowledge of the community to keep this directory alive and accurate. Every submission goes through a manual review queue before it goes live.
