Best Coffee in Carmel Valley, San Diego

Carmel Valley's specialty coffee lineup is small but well-anchored, with Mostra, Achilles, Parakeet, and Bird Rock all running outposts in the North County suburb. Four solid options from some of San Diego's most respected roasters, worth knowing about if you're in the area.
