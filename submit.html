Nominate a Spot

This form is for submitting roasters, multi-roaster cafes, or corrections to the San Diego Specialty Coffee Map.

The goal is to maintain a curated map with detailed notes on each spot — helping navigate San Diego's specialty coffee scene.

What makes a good submission

  • A place you've visited or know firsthand
  • Details about their roast style, coffee program, or barista skillset
  • Roasters and cafes with a genuine specialty focus

Submissions are reviewed before being added. Not all entries will be accepted.
