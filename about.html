What This Is

A community-built directory of specialty coffee in San Diego. Not a generic aggregator, not an algorithm-ranked list. Think of it as a coffee nerd's field guide to the city.

The map covers locations across San Diego neighborhoods: roasters, cafes, and online-only operations shipping from the county. Each listing includes details you won't find on Google Maps: roast style, year established, brew types, amenities, and more. Every location has been reviewed by someone who actually cares about this stuff.

The Community

This project grew out of r/SanDiegoCoffeeBeans, a subreddit for San Diego coffee enthusiasts. What started as a place to swap tips and recommendations turned into a full collaborative map, because the community wanted a real resource, not just Reddit posts.

Each submission is vetted: checking for specialty focus, verifying details, and filling in roast and brew data before anything goes live. The result is a smaller, more reliable directory than open crowdsourcing produces.

How It Works

New locations and edits come in through two places. The Add a Spot page is the more comprehensive option, covering everything from location details to roast profile and amenities. For quick corrections to an existing listing, the Suggest an Edit option on any map card focuses specifically on the coffee details fields. We review each submission, research the spot, and add or update it before anything goes live.

Hours change, roasters open and close. If you spot something out of date, either path gets it in front of someone who can fix it.
